The Best Anime According to Alvin

My Top 3 Anime of All-time

One Piece

One Piece is the best Anime of All-time. One Piece is about the main character, Luffy, that sailed to the ocean to find The Greatest Treasure in The World, One Piece.

Kimetsu No Yaiba

Kimetsu no Yaiba is about the main character, Tanjiro, trying to save his sister, Nezuko, that turned into a demon. So, Tanjiro need to kill the Boss of the Demon.

Naruto

Naruto is about the main character, Naruto, to be the Best Ninja in his village, Konoha.
